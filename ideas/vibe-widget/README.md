# VibeWidget

**Stage:** Seed  
**Created:** 2026-06-19  
**Owner(s):** User  
**One-liner:** A widget-based collaborative social canvas for Gen Z friends to doodle, share avatars, and interact directly from their home screens.

---

## Problem
Gen Z users crave highly visual, casual, and low-friction ways to stay connected with close friends, but existing social platforms are either cluttered with public content, overly structured, or lack integrated collaborative creative tools like live doodling and custom avatars.

## Solution
An interactive home-screen widget and dedicated app designed for close friends. It merges creative features like live doodling and custom bitmoji-style avatars on a shared interactive canvas, allowing friends to interact directly from their home screens.

## Target User
- **Primary:** Gen Z (teens and young adults) who actively share daily media and doodles with tight-knit friend groups.
- **Secondary:** Visual creatives and digital artists looking for collaborative sketching environments.

## Key Assumptions
List the top 3–5 assumptions that, if wrong, would kill this idea:
1. Home screen widgets are the preferred touchpoint for Gen Z's daily interactions.
2. Users will participate in active, daily collaborative doodling rather than just passive viewing.
3. The app can bypass user fatigue from downloading another social app by providing strong home-screen utility immediately.

## Initial Scope (MVP)
A mobile app with a companion home screen widget that lets users:
1. Create/link a small group of friends (up to 5).
2. Live-doodle on a shared canvas.
3. Place customizable avatars (bitmojis) onto the canvas/widget.
4. Auto-update the widget of friends when a change is made.

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Public feed or discovery mechanism (strictly private groups).
- Advanced messaging/chat capabilities (communication is purely through doodles and avatar states).
- Monetization engines (ads, premium avatars).
