# CureIndia

**Stage:** Seed  
**Created:** 2026-06-21  
**Owner(s):** User & Antigravity  
**One-liner:** A platform connecting international patients with accredited Indian hospitals and verified local medical tourism agencies.  

---

## Problem
- Finding trusted, accredited medical facilities and doctors in India is challenging and opaque for foreign patients.
- Navigating the logistics of medical travel (visa, flights, accommodation, local transport, translators) is complex and fragmented.
- Transparent cost estimates are rare, and patients face high risks of hidden fees, markup commissions, and unverified medical agencies.

## Solution
- A centralized marketplace platform that:
  1. Aggregates accredited (JCI/NABH) hospitals with transparent package pricing and physician credentials.
  2. Lists and verifies local medical tourism agencies that offer concierge and hospitality services.
  3. Provides a secure portal for medical record exchange and pre-consultation requests.
  4. Implements a review and rating system to ensure trust and accountability.

## Target User
- **Primary:** Patients from countries with expensive healthcare or long waiting lists (e.g., US, UK, Canada, Middle East, East Africa, Central/South Asia) seeking high-quality, affordable treatments (orthopedics, cardiology, oncology, IVF, dental, cosmetic).
- **Secondary:** NABH/JCI accredited Indian hospitals looking to boost international patient volume, and verified medical tourism agencies seeking lead generation.

## Key Assumptions
List the top 3–5 assumptions that, if wrong, would kill this idea:
1. Hospitals are willing to publish standardized transparent prices or respond to inquiries with fixed-price quotes.
2. Foreign patients are comfortable coordinating major surgical procedures and sharing medical records via an online portal.
3. Verified agencies can maintain a high quality of local concierge services and prevent fraud or negative patient experiences.
4. The regulatory framework allows cross-border sharing of medical data and digital consultation facilitation.

## Initial Scope (MVP)
- A web directory listing 10-15 verified JCI-accredited hospitals in major hubs (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore) for 3 major treatment categories (Cardiology, Orthopedics, Oncology).
- Secure client profile setup with basic medical history and report upload capability.
- Quote request mechanism connecting patients to hospitals and verified agencies.
- Basic rating/review system for hospitals and agencies.

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Direct visa application filing (facilitated by partner agencies).
- Complete telemedicine platform (video consults done via external links/hospital channels).
- In-platform payment escrow and international medical insurance integration.
